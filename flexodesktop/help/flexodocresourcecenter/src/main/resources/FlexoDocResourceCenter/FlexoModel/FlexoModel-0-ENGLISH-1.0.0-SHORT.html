Flexo Business is a process modelling software designed for Business people with intuitive, drag-and-drop graphical tools for process and screen editing.
The strength of the Flexo model lies in a unique combination of a workflow of task distribution (process models), a precise definition of human interactions (user screens) and data accesses (web services, API and database accesses). The Flexo model use a standard graphical representation called the Business Process Modelling Notation (BPMN).
