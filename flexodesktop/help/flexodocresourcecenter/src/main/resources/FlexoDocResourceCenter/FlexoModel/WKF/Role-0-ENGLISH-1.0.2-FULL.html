A Role can represent one user, one function or a group of users or operators according to their rights and duties in the process.
Roles are associated with activities and sub-process activities.

A color is associated with each Role, this gives an operational view on the access rights and the user management.

Roles are defined at the process level and are inherited by sub-proceses. (Common Roles are often defined in the ROOT process to be used in all its sub-processes.)
