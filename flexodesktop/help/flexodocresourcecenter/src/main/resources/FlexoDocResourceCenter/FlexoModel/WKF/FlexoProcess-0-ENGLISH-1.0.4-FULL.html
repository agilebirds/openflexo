Sub-processes are sub-sequences of activities or sub-processes that can be used at different places in the workflow. Sub-processes are associated instances and variables that contain Business data.

The Flexo model encourages the modularity and the reusability by the notion of sub-processes, i.e. sub-sequences of activities or sub-processes that can be reused at different place in the workflow. Subprocesses are organized in a hierarchy. Each sub-process has a father sub-process from which it inherits variables, role and status definition.

There are three types of sub-processes:
  1. The single sub-process regroups a set of activities. They act as subroutines and can be replaced by its set of activities.
  2. The parallel sub-process executes several copies of the sequence of activities in parallel. Parallel sub-processes create embedded business data context that can be different for every instance executed in parallel.
  3. The sequential sub-process executes the sequence of activities several times in sequence. Sequential sub-processes create a different embedded data context for each instances performed in sequence.
