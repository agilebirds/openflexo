Statuses are defined in sub-processes as a linear representation of the differents stages of the process. Statuses are often used in monitoring screen to get an idea of the stagi in which the instance is in the process.
Statuses can be assoiciated with activities, operations and actions. Mostly statuses should only be assoicated with actions. Because normally it should be an action that makes an instance to advance in the process and does change his status.
